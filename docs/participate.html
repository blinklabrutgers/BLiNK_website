Participate in a Study

Do you speak Spanish?

We are recruiting participants for an EEG study on how Spanish speakers process language in real time. If you are interested, we would love to hear from you.

What

An EEG (electroencephalography) study to learn more about how Spanish speakers process language in real time. Participants complete a listening task while wearing an EEG cap. You will be at the lab for approximately 1 hour 30 minutes to 2 hours.

Who

Native Spanish speakers and second-language speakers of Spanish, ages 18 to 40.

Where

Bilingualism: Language in NeuroKognition (BLiNK) Lab
1 Spring Street, New Brunswick, NJ 08901

Pay

Participants receive $30 per hour for completing the study.
